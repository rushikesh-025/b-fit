Tuesday: Back Workout

Deadlift

  1. Stand with feet shoulder-width apart.
  2. Grip the barbell and lift using your hips.
  3. Keep back straight, core tight.
  4. 4 sets of 6–8 reps.

Lat Pulldown

  1. Sit, grip the bar wide.
  2. Pull to chest, squeeze lats.
  3. 3 sets of 10–12 reps.
