Monday: Chest Workout

Flat Bench Press

  1. Lie on a flat bench with feet on the ground.
  2. Grip the bar wider than shoulders.
  3. Lower to chest and press up.
  4. 4 sets of 8–10 reps.

05:00

Incline Dumbbell Press

  1. Sit on incline bench, hold dumbbells.
  2. Press up, then slowly lower.
  3. 3 sets of 10–12 reps.
