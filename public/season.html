Explore Seasonal Plans

Spring (March - May)

Embrace the renewal of spring with light and energizing foods.

Focus: Detoxification, increased energy levels, preparing for warmer months.

Seasonal Produce:

  • Fruits: Mangoes, Amla, Guava, Strawberries, Kiwi
  • Vegetables: Bitter Gourd, Bottle Gourd, Okra, Spinach, Fenugreek, Radish, Carrot, Leafy Greens

Fitness Tips: Incorporate outdoor activities, focus on flexibility and endurance exercises.

Summer (June - August)

Stay hydrated and energized during the hot summer months with refreshing and light options.

Focus: Hydration, maintaining energy in the heat, light and easily digestible foods.

Seasonal Produce:

  • Fruits: Watermelon, Muskmelon, Lychee, Jackfruit, Mangoes, Grapes, Berries
  • Vegetables: Cucumber, Ridge Gourd, Eggplant, Tomatoes, Peppers, Green Beans, Summer Squash

Fitness Tips: Prioritize early morning or late evening workouts, focus on swimming and water-based activities.

Monsoon (September - October)

Strengthen your immunity and enjoy the bounty of the monsoon season.

Focus: Boosting immunity, easily digestible foods, incorporating warming spices.

Seasonal Produce:

  • Fruits: Pomegranate, Papaya, Sweet Lime, Grapes, Apples, Pears, Blueberries
  • Vegetables: Pumpkin, Radish, Carrot, Beetroots, Root Vegetables

Fitness Tips: Focus on indoor workouts, maintain consistency with your routine.

Winter (November - February)

Nourish your body with warming and nutrient-rich foods to stay healthy during the colder months.

Focus: Providing warmth and energy, boosting immunity, incorporating root vegetables.

Seasonal Produce:

  • Fruits: Oranges, Strawberries, Grapes, Pomegranates, Citrus Fruits
  • Vegetables: Mustard Greens, Fenugreek Leaves, Spinach, Carrots, Radish, Beetroot

Fitness Tips: Incorporate strength training to build heat, stay active indoors.
