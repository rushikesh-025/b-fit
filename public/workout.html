Weekly Split

Monday: Chest

Flat Bench Press

  1. Lie on a flat bench with feet on the ground.
  2. Grip the bar wider than shoulders.
  3. Lower to chest and press up.
  4. 4 sets of 8–10 reps.

Incline Dumbbell Press

  1. Sit on incline bench, hold dumbbells.
  2. Press up, then slowly lower.
  3. 3 sets of 10–12 reps.
Try It Yourself

Tuesday: Back

Deadlift

  1. Stand with feet shoulder-width apart.
  2. Grip the barbell and lift using your hips.
  3. Keep back straight, core tight.
  4. 4 sets of 6–8 reps.

Lat Pulldown

  1. Sit, grip the bar wide.
  2. Pull to chest, squeeze lats.
  3. 3 sets of 10–12 reps.
Try It Yourself

Wednesday: Legs

Barbell Squat

  1. Place bar on shoulders behind neck.
  2. Bend knees and lower hips.
  3. Stand back up and repeat.
  4. 4 sets of 10 reps.

Leg Press

  1. Sit in machine, feet on platform.
  2. Push and slowly return.
  3. 3 sets of 12–15 reps.
Try It Yourself

Thursday: Shoulders

Overhead Press

  1. Stand or sit, grip bar at shoulder width.
  2. Press bar overhead and lower slowly.
  3. 4 sets of 8–10 reps.

Lateral Raises

  1. Hold dumbbells at sides.
  2. Raise arms to shoulder level.
  3. 3 sets of 12 reps.
Try It Yourself

Friday: Arms

Barbell Curl

  1. Grip bar with palms facing up.
  2. Curl bar towards chest, lower slowly.
  3. 3 sets of 10–12 reps.

Triceps Pushdown

  1. Grip rope/bar at cable station.
  2. Push down, extend elbows fully.
  3. 3 sets of 12–15 reps.
Try It Yourself

Saturday: Core

Plank

  1. Lie face down, elbows under shoulders.
  2. Lift body and hold straight.
  3. Hold 3 sets for 60 seconds.

Russian Twists

  1. Sit, knees bent, twist side to side.
  2. Use medicine ball if needed.
  3. 3 sets of 20 reps (10 per side).
